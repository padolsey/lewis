A
BILL
TO
Authorise the use for the public service of certain resources for the year ending
31 March 2024 (including income); to authorise the issue out of the
Consolidated Fund of Northern Ireland of certain sums for the service of that
year; to authorise the use of those sums for specified purposes; to authorise
the Department of Finance in Northern Ireland to borrow on the credit of
those sums; and to repeal a spent provision.
